About Us

The Rustic Bakery is your go-to stop for all things freshly baked! Located in the heart of Riyadh, we blend traditional methods with a dash of modern creativity.

Each croissant, every loaf, and all our pastries are crafted to perfection, ensuring flavor and freshness in every bite. Our journey began a decade ago with a single dream: to serve the best bakery treats in the city.

Join our ever-growing family and enjoy the delight of over 500+ satisfied customers who heartily trust our taste!
